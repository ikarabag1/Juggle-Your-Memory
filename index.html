Juggle Your Memory

A Memory Card Game Tribute to strboi

About the Game

Test your memory by matching pairs of cards before time runs out. Flip cards to reveal images, find matching pairs, and clear the board within 2 minutes. Complete the challenge to reveal the full mojo-verse artwork!

How to Play

1

Click any card to flip it and reveal the image

2

Click another card to find its matching pair

3

Match all 8 pairs before the 2-minute timer runs out

4

Complete the game to reveal the mojo-verse artwork
